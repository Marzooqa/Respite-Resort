Memories Aicha

Memories Aicha Hotel is a charming and comfortable accommodation in the heart of Marrakech, Morocco. It offers a blend of traditional Moroccan hospitality, modern amenities, and a peaceful atmosphere. Guests can enjoy cozy rooms, a beautiful courtyard, and easy access to the city's attractions.

The Memories Aicha Luxury Camp consists of 55 tents, nestled in the heart of the Wadi Rum. Bordered by rugged mountains and the stunning Arabian desert, this camp lets you wake up in the wild outdoors without compromising an ounce of luxury. Expect the same amenities and facilities as you would have in a top-class hotel.
